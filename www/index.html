What is NetPass?

In the current state of the world, the 3DS is, sadly, on decline. That makes getting StreetPasses harder and harder, due to fewer and fewer people taking their 3DS with them on a walk. This is where NetPass comes in!

Unlike StreetPass, NetPass works over the internet. Upon opening NetPass, you can pick various locations to go to, i.e. the train station, or a town plaza. Upon entering a location, you get passes of others who are in the same location! And, while you are at the location, others who enter it can get passes with you. But beware! You can only switch locations once every 10 hours!

Rules

Below you will find basic rules for using NetPass. Breaking them could result in a ban!

1. Be respectful and inclusive Be nice to people and treat others with respect. No harassment, sexism, racism, hate speech, homophobia, transphobia, or anything of the sort. Be inclusive of others and their beliefs; we want this place to be welcome to all.

2. Keep things PG-13 No NSFW, gore or obscene content. This applies to text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content. Swearing is allowed, but should be kept to a minimum and not be used as a way to insult/harm others.

3. No piracy Do not discuss piracy or anything relating to piracy. Do not ask how to pirate games/updates/dlcs, firmware dumps, SDK's for consoles, etc. Do not share, discuss, hint or guide towards downloads links or tools which promote or encourage the use of piracy.

4. Be careful with sensitive data No doxxing, malware, viruses, IP grabbers, media intended to crash the Discord client or brick/crash any system. Do not impersonate others & do not send other people's private information, this also applies to the sender sending their own private info.

5. No advertising or spam No unsolicited spam, scams, advertising, or (self-) promotion in the Discord server or in DMs. Posting links to things you’ve made is fine if it is relevant to the conversation.No unsolicited spam, scams, advertising, or corporate promotion in NetPass. Links to social media and things you’ve made are fine as long as it is personal work.

6. Use your common sense If you're hesitant to post something, it's better to ask for clarification or to not post it. Finding loopholes or trying to stay 'borderline' within the rules will be treated as breaking them. This also includes using channels for their intended purposes.
