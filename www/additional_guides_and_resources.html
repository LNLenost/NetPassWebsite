Additional Guides

Mii Plaza: Meet 100 People in a day

  • Continue using NetPass, but stop clearing the Mii Plaza entrance. NetPass will start storing excess StreetPasses on its server for up to 15 days
  • Once you're decently confident NetPass has accumulated 100 StreetPasses clear the Mii Plaza entrance
  • Open NetPass, let it download the next 10 StreetPasses, open Mii Plaza, clear the entrance and repeat
