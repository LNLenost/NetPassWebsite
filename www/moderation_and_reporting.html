Moderation and Reporting

How do I report unwanted/inappropriate content?

If you found something that breaks one of our Rules, or believe you found something harmful not yet covered by our rules you can send a report using the following steps:

  1. In your in-app settings, there should be a report button.
  2. On the report screen, you can scroll and choose who to report. Clicking on a name will show the offender’s name and associated StreetPass Data (including a preview of their SwapNote drawing).
    • This will NOT YET send the report! You can safely check the data and back out by pressing B if it's the wrong person.
  3. Once you have selected the offender, click the A button to begin writing a report. Please include as much context as you can fit in the text box.

If you cancel the report at any of the above steps it will not be sent. If you submit your written report it will be sent to the mod panel, which mods will validate and give punishment if necessary. All reports are manually reviewed, so please avoid submitting fake reports/spam; those who do will be punished.
