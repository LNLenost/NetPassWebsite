What is NetPass?

In the current state of the world, the 3DS is, sadly, on decline. That makes getting StreetPasses harder and harder, due to fewer and fewer people taking their 3DS with them on a walk. This is where NetPass comes in!

Unlike StreetPass, NetPass works over the internet. Upon opening NetPass, you can pick various locations to go to, i.e. the train station, or a town plaza. Upon entering a location, you get passes of others who are in the same location! And, while you are at the location, others who enter it can get passes with you. But beware! You can only switch locations once every 10 hours!

How to Install NetPass

Follow these steps to install NetPass on your 3DS!

  1. Open FBI on your 3DS.
  2. Scan the QR code below.
  3. Install the file and launch NetPass. You are good to go now!

How to Use

Upon opening NetPass, you can pick various locations to go to, i.e. the train station, or a town plaza. Upon entering a location, you get passes of others who are in the same location! And, while you are at the location, others who enter it can get passes with you. But beware! You can only switch locations once every 10 hours! The Back Alley is a way to get more passes by paying using Coins. You can choose the game you want to receive the StreetPass and you will instantly receive it! The first one of the day is free :)

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work on all 3DS models?

Yes! NetPass works on all models. They just need custom firmware!

Will I get banned or something?

No, NetPass has nothing to do with online play, Pretendo, or Nintendo servers. So you won't get banned! :D
