Frequently Asked Questions

What is NetPass?

NetPass is a homebrew app that simulates StreetPass connections over the internet.

Does it work on all 3DS models?

Yes, NetPass works on all models with custom firmware.

Is this legal?

NetPass is a homebrew app, and using homebrew is allowed on modded 3DS systems.
